INNOVATION CAMP SCHEDULE

January 14-18

Join us for our first camp of the year. The goal of this innovation camp is to inprint the mindset of an innovator in the participants. The camp will run from the 14th to the 18th of January, 17-21 EST.

February 22-25

This camp will be focused around hands-on innovation. The participants will learn the basics of realizing and bringing their imagination to life. This camp will teach woodworking, metalworking and basic electical engineering.

March 12-16

The March camp will be focused around your imagination. The participants will learn the basics of how to think like an innovator.

April 2-6

The goal of this innovation camp is to futher inprint the mindset of an innovator in the participants. The participants will be asked to invent a solution to a problem they have in their daily life. The camp will run from the 2nd to the 6th of May, 17-21 EST.

May 17-22

Our only zoom-only innovation camp takes place between 17-21 EST in June. This camp will feature many world-renowned guest speakers, who will further inspire and guide the participants to become great innovators.

June 12-13

Our shortest, but maybe most important camp will run for two days in the middle of June. This camp is focused on teaching the participants how to take the next step, how to further develop and branch out their products and innovations to the world.

July 15-19

The July camp has for many years been a meeting ground for our innovators and experts in important fields, and this year is no different. We will get you in touch with the market-leading experts in whichever field you need to further build your innovation.

August 6-8

The August camp will be focused around your imagination. The participants will learn the basics of how to think like an innovator.

September 13-15

This camp will be focused around hands-on innovation. The participants will learn the basics of realizing and bringing their imagination to life. This camp will teach woodworking, metalworking and basic electical engineering.
